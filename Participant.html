PARTICIPANT BACKGROUND

Our usability study involved six participants from the Partido State University community. By splitting the group between those familiar with digital reservations and those completely new to them, we gained a 360-degree view of the MedCare experience. All sessions were recorded on-site at the Learning Center to capture authentic user behavior and environmental context.
