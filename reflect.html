Reflections

💡 What We Learned

Through the usability testing process, we learned how crucial user-centered design is in developing effective healthcare systems. Even small interface issues can significantly affect user experience and task completion.

👥 Team Collaboration

Working as a team allowed us to gather diverse perspectives and insights. Each member contributed unique ideas, which improved both our evaluation process and final outcomes.

⚙️ Challenges Encountered

One of the main challenges was ensuring that all usability issues were properly identified and categorized. Time constraints and coordinating schedules also posed difficulties.

🚀 Future Improvements

If given more time, we would expand testing with more participants and further refine the prototype based on additional feedback to enhance overall usability.
